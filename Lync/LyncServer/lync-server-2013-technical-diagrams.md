## <a name="tips-for-printing-posters"></a><span data-ttu-id="68550-179">列印海報的秘訣</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-179">Tips for printing posters</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="68550-180">如果您有繪圖機，則可以用海報的完整大小來列印這些海報。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-180">If you have a plotter, you can print these posters in their full size.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="68550-181">如果您沒有繪圖器，請使用下列步驟在較小的紙上列印。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-181">If you don't have plotter, use the following steps to print on smaller paper.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="68550-182">**在較小的紙張上列印海報**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-182">**Print posters on smaller paper**</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="68550-183">在 Visio 中開啟海報。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-183">Open the poster in Visio.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="68550-184">在 [檔案]\*\*\*\* 功能表上，按一下 [版面設定]\*\*\*\*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-184">On the **File** menu, click **Page Setup**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="68550-185">在 [列印設定]\*\*\*\* 索引標籤的 [印表機紙張]\*\*\*\* 區段中，選取想要在其上列印的紙張大小。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-185">On the **Print Setup** tab, in the **Printer paper** section, select the size of paper you want to print on.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="68550-186">在 [列印設定]\*\*\*\* 索引標籤的 [列印比例]\*\*\*\* 區段中，按一下 [調整成]\*\*\*\*，然後輸入 [1 sheet across by 1 sheet down]\*\*\*\*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-186">On the **Print Setup** tab, in the **Print zoom** section, click **Fit to**, and then enter **1 sheet across by 1 sheet down**.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="68550-187">在 [紙張大小]\*\*\*\* 索引標籤上，按一下 [大小符合繪圖內容]\*\*\*\*，然後再按一下 [確定]\*\*\*\*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-187">On the **Page Size** tab, click **Size to fit drawing contents**, and then click **OK**.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="68550-188">在 [檔案]\*\*\*\* 功能表上，按一下 [列印]\*\*\*\*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68550-188">On the **File** menu, click **Print**.</span></span>
