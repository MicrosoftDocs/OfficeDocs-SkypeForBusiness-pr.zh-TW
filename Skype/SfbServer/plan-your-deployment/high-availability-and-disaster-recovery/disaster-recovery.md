<span data-ttu-id="6b761-127">即使兩個前端池之間的備份關聯必須是1:1 和對稱式, 每個前端池也可以是任何數量的 Survivable 分支裝置的備份註冊機構。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6b761-127">Even though backup relationships between two Front End pools must be 1:1 and symmetrical, each Front End pool can still also be the backup registrar for any number of Survivable Branch Appliances.</span></span>

## <a name="recovery-time-for-pool-failover-and-pool-failback"></a><span data-ttu-id="6b761-130">集區容錯回復與集區容錯移轉的復原時間</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6b761-130">Recovery time for pool failover and pool failback</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6b761-131">針對池容錯移轉和池回切, 「恢復時間目標」 (RTO) 的工程目標是15-20 分鐘。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6b761-131">For pool failover and pool failback, the engineering target for recovery time objective (RTO) is 15-20 minutes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6b761-132">這是發生容錯移轉所需的時間, 在管理員決定發生災難並開始容錯移轉程式後。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6b761-132">This is the time required for the failover to happen, after administrators have determined there was a disaster and started the failover procedures.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6b761-133">它不會包含管理員評估情況和作出決定的時間, 也不會包含使用者在容錯移轉完成後再次登入的時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6b761-133">It does not include the time for administrators to assess the situation and make a decision, nor does it include the time for users to sign in again after failover is complete.</span></span>
