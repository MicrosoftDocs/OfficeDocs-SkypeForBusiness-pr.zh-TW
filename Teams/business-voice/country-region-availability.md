<span data-ttu-id="4c8db-108">在 **具有** 通話方案和快速入門精靈的國家與地區，您可以從 Microsoft 購買 Business Voice，然後設定電話號碼，或將現有的電話號碼轉移到 Microsoft。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4c8db-108">In countries and regions **with** support for Calling Plans and the Getting Started Wizard, you can buy Business Voice from Microsoft and then set up or transfer your existing phone numbers to Microsoft.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4c8db-109">這樣做可讓您在 Microsoft 365 中管理所有電話號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4c8db-109">Doing this will let you manage all of your phone numbers in Microsoft 365.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4c8db-110">完成移轉至 Business Voice 之後：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4c8db-110">After your move to Business Voice is finished:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="4c8db-118">您的電信業者不會變更。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4c8db-118">Your telephone provider doesn't change.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4c8db-119">所有當地、長途和免付費電話會由您現有的協力廠商電信業者計費。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4c8db-119">All local, long distance, and toll-free charges are billed by your existing third-party telephone provider.</span></span>
