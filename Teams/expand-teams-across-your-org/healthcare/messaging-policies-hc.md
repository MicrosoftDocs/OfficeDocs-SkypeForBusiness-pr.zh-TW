# <a name="secure-messaging-for-healthcare-organizations"></a><span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-103">適用於醫療保健組織的安全郵件傳遞</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-103">Secure Messaging for healthcare organizations</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-104">訊息原則可用來控制哪些聊天和頻道訊息功能可供 Microsoft Teams 中的使用者使用，且屬於醫療保健組織 (例如醫院、診所或醫生辦公室) 之安全訊息的整體部署之一部分，其中要以及時的方式取得訊息並採取動作非常重要，知道重要訊息何時被讀取也一樣重要。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-104">Messaging policies are used to control which chat and channel messaging features are available to users in Microsoft Teams, and are part of the overall deployment of Secure Messaging for healthcare organizations like Hospitals, clinics, or doctor's offices, where having a message picked up and acted upon in a timely manner is crucial, as is knowing when crucial messages are read.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-105">您可以使用全域 (全組織預設值) 原則，或是為組織中的人員建立一或多個自訂訊息原則。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-105">You can use the global (Org-wide default) policy or create one or more custom messaging policies for people in your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-106">除非您建立並指派自訂原則，否則組織中的使用者將會自動取得全域原則。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-106">Users in your organization will automatically get the global policy unless you create and assign a custom policy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-107">建立自訂原則之後，請為它指派組織中的一個使用者或一組使用者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-107">After you create a custom policy, assign it a user or groups of users in your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-108">例如，您可以選擇只允許特定工作角色使用這些功能 (可能只有醫生和護士)，而允許其他工作者 (例如清潔或廚房人員) 可獲得一組有限的功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-108">For example, you may choose to only allow certain job roles to use these features (perhaps doctors and nurses only) and other workers (like the janitorial or kitchen staff) to get a more limited set of features.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-109">請自行決定您組織的需求，這裡的指引頂多是建議。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-109">Decide for yourself what needs your organization has, the guidance here is at most a suggestion.</span></span>

## <a name="read-receipts"></a><span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-117">讀取回條</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-117">Read receipts</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-121">針對醫療保健應用程式，選擇 [使用者控制 **]** 或 [針對每個人開啟 **]**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-121">For Healthcare applications, choose either **User controlled** or **On for everyone**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-122">請注意，使用 [針對每個人開啟 **]** 設定時，為整個租用戶設定回條的唯一方法，就是讓整個租用戶只有一個訊息原則 (名為「全域 (全組織預設值)」的預設原則)，或讓租用戶中所有的訊息原則都使用相同的回條設定。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-122">Be aware that when using the **On for everyone** setting, the only way to set receipts for the whole  tenant is either to have only one messaging policy for the whole tenant (the default policy named "Global (Org-wide Default)") or to have all messaging policies in the tenant use the same settings for receipts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-123">當此功能對 [針對每個人開啟 **]** 啟用時，讀信回條功能最為有效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-123">The read receipts feature is most effective when the feature is enabled to **On for everyone**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-128">*有讀取回條的使用範例：* 高風險病患 Jakob Roth 獲接納可進入醫院。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-128">*Usage example with read receipts:* Jakob Roth, a high risk patient, is admitted to the hospital.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-129">Sofia Krause 是一名護士，她是醫療工作者 (包括不同專家) 之跨學科小組 (IDT) 的一員，被指派為負責此病患的主要照護協調員。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-129">Sofia Krause is a nurse working as part of the inter-disciplinary team (IDT) of medical workers, including different specialists, is assigned as the primary care coordinator in charge of this patient.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-130">Sofia 開始與將與病患合作以協調照護的一組醫生和其他護士進行群組聊天，並開始進行緊急情況分級。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-130">Sofia starts a group chat with a set of doctors and other nurses who will be working with the patient to coordinate care and starts an emergency triage.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-131">護士和醫生在整個照護協調程序中，會針對該病患的照護計劃進行溝通和共同作業。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-131">The nurses and doctors communicate and collaborate over the patient's care plan throughout the care coordination process.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-132">重要和緊急訊息會透過一對一和群組聊天交談來傳送。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-132">Important and urgent messages are sent through 1:1 and group chat conversations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-133">Sofia 使用讀取回條功能來判斷所傳送要求支援的訊息是否已傳遞並由目標醫生或護士讀取。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-133">Sofia uses the read receipts functionality to determine if messages sent requesting support are delivered and read by the targeted physicians or nurses.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1cd1-134">Jakob 的病患成果接近最理想狀態，而由於他的健康小組能順暢地溝通，他便更早出院了。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1cd1-134">Jakob's patient outcomes are near-optimal and he goes home sooner because his health team communicates smoothly.</span></span>
