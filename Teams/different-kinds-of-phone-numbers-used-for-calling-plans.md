<span data-ttu-id="6e847-104">Microsoft 團隊根據您想要使用電話號碼的目的，使用不同的電話號碼類型。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-104">Microsoft Teams uses different telephone number types depending on the purpose for which you want to use the phone number.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-105">團隊使用**使用者**編號（可指派給組織中的使用者），以及指派給服務的**服務**號碼（例如音訊會議、自動語音應答或通話佇列）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-105">Teams uses **user** numbers, which can be assigned to users in your organization, and **service** numbers, which are assigned to services such as Audio Conferencing, auto attendants, or call queues.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-106">服務電話號碼的*並行通話容量*比使用者號碼還大。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-106">Service phone numbers have a *higher concurrent call capacity*  than user numbers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-107">您可以使用服務電話號碼，但會依國家/地區和號碼類型（無論是付費或免費電話號碼）而有所不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-107">Service phone numbers are available but will vary by country/region and the type of number (whether it's a toll or toll-free number).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-108">如果您需要其他或其他數位類型，而不是 Microsoft 團隊系統管理中心所顯示的數位，您可以將電話號碼要求提交給[PSTN 服務台](manage-phone-numbers-for-your-organization/contact-pstn-service-desk.md)說明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-108">If you need additional or other number types other than those numbers seen in the Microsoft Teams admin center, you can submit a phone number request to the [PSTN service desk help](manage-phone-numbers-for-your-organization/contact-pstn-service-desk.md).</span></span>
  
- <span data-ttu-id="6e847-109">**使用者編號**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-109">**User numbers**</span></span>
    
    <span data-ttu-id="6e847-110">使用者編號會指派給使用者，且有兩種類型：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-110">User numbers are assigned to users, and there are two kinds:</span></span>
    
  - <span data-ttu-id="6e847-111">**地理位置編號**地理數位具有地理區域的關聯性，而且是最常見的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-111">**Geographic numbers** Geographic numbers have a relationship to a geographic area and are the most common.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-112">例如，在大多數情況下，地理電話號碼只能在國家/地區的特定位址、城市、州或地區內使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-112">For example, geographic phone numbers in most cases can only be used within a certain address, city, state, or region of the country.</span></span>
    
  - <span data-ttu-id="6e847-113">**非地理數位**非地理數位是國家/地區內地理區域之間沒有關聯的國家或地區數位。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-113">**Non-geographic numbers** Non-geographic numbers are national numbers that don't have a relationship to a geographic area within a country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-114">例如，當您從國家/地區內的任何位置撥打號碼時，非地理編號通常會有相同的成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-114">For example, non-geographic numbers often have the same cost when calling the number from anywhere within the country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-115">此外，有些國家（例如丹麥）只有沒有可用的非地理數位。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-115">Also, some countries, such as Denmark, only have non-geographic numbers available.</span></span>
    
- <span data-ttu-id="6e847-116">**服務號碼**服務號碼有數種不同的編號類型，而且可用性因國家/地區而異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-116">**Service numbers** Service numbers are available in several different number types, and availability does vary by country/region.</span></span>
    
  - <span data-ttu-id="6e847-117">**收費服務號碼**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-117">**Toll service numbers**</span></span>

      - <span data-ttu-id="6e847-119">**地理位置編號**地理編號有地理區域的關聯性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-119">**Geographic numbers** Geographic numbers have a relationship to a geographic area.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-120">例如，在大多數情況下，地理電話號碼只能在國家/地區的特定位址、城市、州或地區內使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-120">For example, geographic phone numbers in most cases can only be used within a certain address, city, state, or region of the country.</span></span>
        
      - <span data-ttu-id="6e847-121">**非地理數位**非地理數位是國家/地區內地理區域之間沒有關聯的國家或地區數位。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-121">**Non-geographic numbers** Non-geographic numbers are national numbers that don't have a relationship to a geographic area within a country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-122">例如，當您從國家/地區內的任何位置撥打號碼時，非地理編號通常會有相同的成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-122">For example, non-geographic numbers often have the same cost when calling the number from anywhere within the country/region.</span></span>
   
  - <span data-ttu-id="6e847-123">**免付費服務號碼**這些服務號碼通常不會對來電者收取付費成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-123">**Toll-free service numbers** These service numbers don't typically incur a toll cost to the caller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e847-124">團隊在超過60個國家/地區提供國內免付費電話號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e847-124">Teams provides national toll-free numbers in over 60 countries/regions.</span></span>
