- <span data-ttu-id="62b55-109">**使用者編號**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62b55-109">**User numbers**</span></span>
    
    <span data-ttu-id="62b55-110">使用者編號會指派給使用者，而且有兩種：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62b55-110">User numbers are assigned to users, and there are two kinds:</span></span>

      - <span data-ttu-id="62b55-121">**非地理號碼** 非地理數位是國家/地區內與地理區域沒有關系的國家號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62b55-121">**Non-geographic numbers** Non-geographic numbers are national numbers that don't have a relationship to a geographic area within a country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="62b55-122">例如，從國家/地區內任何位置撥打號碼時，非地理號碼的成本通常相同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62b55-122">For example, non-geographic numbers often have the same cost when calling the number from anywhere within the country/region.</span></span>
   
  - <span data-ttu-id="62b55-123">**免付費服務號碼** 這些服務號碼通常不會向來電者收取付費費用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62b55-123">**Toll-free service numbers** These service numbers don't typically incur a toll cost to the caller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="62b55-124">Teams提供超過 60 個國家/地區的免付費電話號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62b55-124">Teams provides national toll-free numbers in over 60 countries/regions.</span></span>
