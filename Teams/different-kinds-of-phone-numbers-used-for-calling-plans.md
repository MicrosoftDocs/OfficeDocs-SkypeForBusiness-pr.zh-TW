# <a name="different-kinds-of-phone-numbers-used-for-calling-plans"></a><span data-ttu-id="9070f-103">用於通話方案的各種電話號碼</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-103">Different kinds of phone numbers used for Calling Plans</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9070f-104">Microsoft Teams 會根據您想要使用電話號碼的用途，使用不同的電話號碼類型。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-104">Microsoft Teams uses different telephone number types depending on the purpose for which you want to use the phone number.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-105">Teams **會** 使用使用者號碼，可指派給貴組織的使用者，以及服務號碼，這些號碼會指派給音訊會議、自動語音服務或通話佇列等服務。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-105">Teams uses **user** numbers, which can be assigned to users in your organization, and **service** numbers, which are assigned to services such as Audio Conferencing, auto attendants, or call queues.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-106">服務電話號碼的並行 *通話容量高於*  使用者號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-106">Service phone numbers have a *higher concurrent call capacity*  than user numbers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-107">服務電話號碼可供使用，但會依國家/地區及號碼類型 (無論是付費或免付費號碼) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-107">Service phone numbers are available but will vary by country/region and the type of number (whether it's a toll or toll-free number).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-108">如果您需要 Microsoft Teams 系統管理中心所看到號碼外的其他號碼類型或其他號碼類型，您可以提交電話號碼要求給 [PSTN 服務台協助](manage-phone-numbers-for-your-organization/contact-pstn-service-desk.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-108">If you need additional or other number types other than those numbers seen in the Microsoft Teams admin center, you can submit a phone number request to the [PSTN service desk help](manage-phone-numbers-for-your-organization/contact-pstn-service-desk.md).</span></span>
  
- <span data-ttu-id="9070f-109">**使用者編號**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-109">**User numbers**</span></span>
    
    <span data-ttu-id="9070f-110">使用者編號會指派給使用者，而且有兩種：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-110">User numbers are assigned to users, and there are two kinds:</span></span>
    
  - <span data-ttu-id="9070f-111">**地理號碼** 地理數位與地理區域有關系，且最常見。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-111">**Geographic numbers** Geographic numbers have a relationship to a geographic area and are the most common.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-112">例如，在大多數情況下，地理位置電話號碼只能在國家/地區的特定位址、城市、省/市或地區內使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-112">For example, geographic phone numbers in most cases can only be used within a certain address, city, state, or region of the country.</span></span>
    
  - <span data-ttu-id="9070f-113">**非地理號碼** 非地理數位是國家/地區內與地理區域沒有關系的國家號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-113">**Non-geographic numbers** Non-geographic numbers are national numbers that don't have a relationship to a geographic area within a country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-114">例如，從國家/地區內任何位置撥打號碼時，非地理號碼的成本通常相同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-114">For example, non-geographic numbers often have the same cost when calling the number from anywhere within the country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-115">此外，部分國家 /地區 ，例如丹麥，僅提供非地理號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-115">Also, some countries, such as Denmark, only have non-geographic numbers available.</span></span>
    
- <span data-ttu-id="9070f-116">**服務編號** 服務號碼提供數種不同的號碼類型，且可用性會因國家/地區而異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-116">**Service numbers** Service numbers are available in several different number types, and availability does vary by country/region.</span></span>
    
  - <span data-ttu-id="9070f-117">**付費服務號碼**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-117">**Toll service numbers**</span></span>
    
    <span data-ttu-id="9070f-118">付費服務號碼可能會向來電者收取付費費用，而且有兩種：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-118">Toll service numbers may incur a toll cost to the caller, and there are two kinds:</span></span>
    
      - <span data-ttu-id="9070f-119">**地理號碼** 地理數位與地理區域有關系。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-119">**Geographic numbers** Geographic numbers have a relationship to a geographic area.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-120">例如，在大多數情況下，地理位置電話號碼只能在國家/地區的特定位址、城市、省/市或地區內使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-120">For example, geographic phone numbers in most cases can only be used within a certain address, city, state, or region of the country.</span></span>
        
      - <span data-ttu-id="9070f-121">**非地理號碼** 非地理數位是國家/地區內與地理區域沒有關系的國家號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-121">**Non-geographic numbers** Non-geographic numbers are national numbers that don't have a relationship to a geographic area within a country/region.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-122">例如，從國家/地區內任何位置撥打號碼時，非地理號碼的成本通常相同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-122">For example, non-geographic numbers often have the same cost when calling the number from anywhere within the country/region.</span></span>
   
  - <span data-ttu-id="9070f-123">**免付費服務號碼** 這些服務號碼通常不會向來電者收取付費費用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-123">**Toll-free service numbers** These service numbers don't typically incur a toll cost to the caller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9070f-124">Teams 提供超過 60 個國家/地區的免付費號碼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-124">Teams provides national toll-free numbers in over 60 countries/regions.</span></span>
    
    > [!CAUTION]
    > <span data-ttu-id="9070f-125">部分國家/地區及原始號碼類型 ，例如來自行動電話的通話，在某些情況下，可能會向來電者收取付費費用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9070f-125">Some countries/regions and originating number types, such as calls originating from mobile phones, may in some cases incur a toll cost to the caller.</span></span>
