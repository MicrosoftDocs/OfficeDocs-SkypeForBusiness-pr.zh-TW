## <a name="interoperability-of-teams-and-skype-for-business"></a><span data-ttu-id="ce15f-170">團隊與商務用 Skype 的互通性</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ce15f-170">Interoperability of Teams and Skype for Business</span></span>
