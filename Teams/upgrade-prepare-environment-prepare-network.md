- <span data-ttu-id="1cf4a-239">**延遲：** 這是取得 IP 資料包從點 A 到網路上的點 B 所需的時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1cf4a-239">**Latency:** This is the time it takes to get an IP packet from point A to point B on the network.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1cf4a-240">此網路傳播延遲實質上會與兩個點之間的實際距離和光線速度之間的距離產生關聯，包括不同的路由器所佔用的額外負荷。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1cf4a-240">This network propagation delay is essentially tied to physical distance between the two points and the speed of light, including additional overhead taken by the various routers in between.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1cf4a-241">延遲是以單向或往返時間的方式來測量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1cf4a-241">Latency is measured as one-way or round-trip time.</span></span>
